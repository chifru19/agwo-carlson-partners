Professional Profile

Ndam Carlson Agwo | Managing Partner

Ndam Carlson Agwo is a distinguished Barrister and Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Cameroon and the Federal Supreme Court of Nigeria. With extensive experience navigating Cameroon’s bijural system, he has successfully managed complex litigation and corporate transactions across both Anglophone and Francophone jurisdictions.

Beyond his legal practice, Mr. Agwo is a government-trained Grade 1 Teacher, bridging the gap between legal advocacy and social empowerment. He actively fights systemic injustice, providing pro bono services for marginalized groups, including the elderly, refugees, and those facing poverty.
